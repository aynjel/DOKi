How to approve Final Diagnosis & Medical Certificate?
  Final Diagnosis & Med Cert Approval

1. Log in to DOKi app. Then, tap the inbox icon at the upper right portion of the page.

2. Tap “FOR APPROVAL” tab.

3. If no revisions are needed, tap “View & Sign” button.

4. Preview of Medical Certificate is displayed. Tap “Sign and Approve” button to proceed.

Acknowledge the Consent Form. Then, tap SAVE button to proceed.

Affix your electronic signature. Then, tap Save button.

Signatures are saved so it can be reused in the succeeding approvals. To reuse, tap “Yes” button. Otherwise, tap “Sign Again” and follow the previous step.

Approved Final Diagnosis will be transferred to “APPROVED” tab, tap to check.

If revision is necessary, tap “Send for Revision” button.

Specify the revision/s and tap “Save”.

It will then be displayed under “FOR REVISION” tab, tap to check.

Once revised and resubmitted for approval thru HIS, an SMS notification is sent to AP.

It will be displayed under “FOR APPROVAL” tab with “Revised” tagging. Tap to check and approve.
